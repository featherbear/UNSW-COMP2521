When do hunters get sent to JM St Joseph and St Mary?
Relevant parts of the Rules §Hunter Action:

When a hunter enters a city, the Hunter encounters, in sequence, all Traps for that city which are in the current trail, any immature Vampires, and at last Dracula himself. Each of these events occur in turn until either the Hunter is reduced to 0 or less life points or all the encounters have occurred. At the point when they reach 0 life points, they are instantly automagically teleported to JM St Joseph and St Mary’s, and can do nothing else for the remainder of their turn.

Essentially: when a hunter enters a city, if they die during the encounters, they immediately go to JM, can’t do anything for that turn (they still have 0 life points), and in the following turn, they can move again.
